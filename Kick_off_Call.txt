## Social Engineering Test Kick-off Discussion/Questions

## Review scope, authorization form

## Questons

- At a high-level can you describe the employees that you'd like targeted for the phishing campaign?  

- Can you describe how mail is presented to users?  Microsoft Outlook?
    - Is there an email security product?

- What is the objective of the phishing campaign?
    - Would you like me to attempt to steal credentials, gain remote access to devices, or steal other sensitive data?
    
- Would you like me to perform vishing as well?

- Would you like to provide me with emails and phone numbers or would you like me to conduct recon to discover this material?

- How often and through which mechanism would you like me to communicate updates?
